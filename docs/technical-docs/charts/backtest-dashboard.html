PANIK — Black-Swan Backtest

Liquidation early-warning, validated on real crashes · 2 chains · 4 protocols · 6 crises · ~7,700 real wallets

A position is liquidated when its health factor (HF) reaches ~1.0; PANIK warns CRITICAL earlier. We reconstruct exact on-chain HFs for liquidated and surviving borrowers and replay them through the engine — measuring recall (caught the doomed) and false-alarm (cried wolf on survivors).
