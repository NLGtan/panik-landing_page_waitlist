Old system vs PANIK: hours of warning before liquidation

More time = more options. With 44 hours of warning you can calmly add collateral, partially repay the loan, or exit at a reasonable price. With 17 hours — especially overnight — many people simply miss the window entirely.

Bottom line

In the biggest ETH crash on record, PANIK caught 88 out of 100 positions that were heading for liquidation — and delivered warnings a median of 44 hours early instead of 17. The $40M whale that made headlines got only 17 hours under the old system. With PANIK, that position would have received its warning 44 hours out. About 27 out of 100 warnings went to positions that ultimately survived — in a crash that severe, being close to the edge is genuinely dangerous even if you pull through.
