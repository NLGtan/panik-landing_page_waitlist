FAQ Accordion

What is a PANIK Risk Score?

It is a proprietary 0–100 index calculating position health, asset volatility deviation, smart contract exploits safety margins, and systemic blockchain congestion state. High-values mean high-urgency actions must protect deposits.

How do personal risk parameters calibrate scoring?

During orientation, you classify target limits. Standard defaults load a Conservative baseline. An elevated score activates and warns you based on your personal settings rather than uniform averages.

Does PANIK custody user funds?

Never. We maintain telemetry services. All capital actions occur inside Compound, Aave, and Moonwell core consensus files. We only construct client-side transaction payloads for user extensions to verify and authorize.
